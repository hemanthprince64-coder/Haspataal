# Project: Haspataal

## Vision
India's smart hospital assistance platform connecting patients with local hospitals for outpatient consultations, diagnostic services, and inpatient services. Haspataal aims to provide "Assistance & Accuracy in Healthcare" by bridging the gap between patients and medical providers.

## Core Value Propositions
- **Assistance:** End-to-end guidance for patients.
- **Accuracy:** Reliable healthcare decisions and referrals.
- **Accessibility:** Easy access to records and support.
- **Accountability:** Transparent processes.

## Key Features
- **Doctor Discovery:** Find specialists by city and category.
- **Hospital Listing:** View hospital details and doctor availability.
- **OPD Scheduling:** Book consultations.
- **Health Records:** Secure digital storage for medical history.

## Technical Goals
- High performance Next.js application.
- Secure, scalable database (Prisma + SQL).
- Mobile-responsive design (Mobile-first approach).
